Ideal Weight

Being a healthy weight has important benefits, not only on how you feel, but also for lowering your risk of heart disease,Being overweight or obese can lead to a multitude of health issues from heart disease Being a healthy weight means not being under or overweight. A BMI of 20-25 is considered healthy for most adults.

Being underweight may be associated with lack of some vitamins and minerals that can affect important body functions, such as your immune response to infection and fertility or it can lead to a multitude of health issues from heart disease to bone problems.

This free ideal weight calculator estimates ideal healthy bodyweight based on weight, and height (BMI) formulas.
